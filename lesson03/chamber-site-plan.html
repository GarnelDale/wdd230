Site Plan

Site Name

Ald'ruhn Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

To provide a network of communication for the various merchants of Ald'ruhn and to provide a resource of visitors of the city to find the services they are looking for. To do this a join service will be necessary for merchants of Ald'ruhn and a listing of all registered merchants with locations will be required for visitors to the city.

Scenarios

Color Schema

The color for the header, footer, and major page divisions will be #7b2210

The main color for the page will be #e0b09f

Typography

The font for the headings will be Magic Card Normal as that is the primary font of Morrowind, the game where Ald'ruhn is from.

The font for the primary content of the page will be Nanum Gothic, as it is significantly easier to read and resembles to font of the console from Morrowind.
