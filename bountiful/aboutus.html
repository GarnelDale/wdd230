About Us

Carlsbad

Originally the homelands of the Luise√±o Indians, the "Village by the Sea" was settled by Spanish missions starting in the late 18th century. Shortly after 1882, it was discovered that the fresh well water in the area was similar in composition to the famous spas of Europe, earning Carlsbad its name as a nod to Karlsbad in Bohemia. Carlsbad is known for its beaches, farms, and many other local attractions, including the Flower Fields at Carlsbad Ranch.

The Flower Fields

The flower fields were established over 60 years ago and are an annual tourist site showcasing 55 acres of patterned flowers. These flowers announce the arrival of spring as they bloom from early March to early May each year. These fields have become a defining part of the local heritage of Carlsbad.
